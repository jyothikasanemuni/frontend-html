ABOUT NANI

Ghanta Naveen Babu (born 24 February 1984), known professionally as Nani, is anIndian actor and producer who predominantly works in Telugu films . One of the highest-paid and most popular Telugu actors, Nani is a recipient of several accolades including two Nandi Awards, three Filmfare Awards South and four SIIMA Awards .Nani made his debut in 2008 starring in the romantic comedy Ashta Chamma, a box office success and attained his breakthrough with Ala Modalaindi (2011). He went on to star in commercially successful films such as Pilla Zamindar (2011), Eega (2012) and Yevade Subramanyam (2015). He won the Nandi Award for Best Actor for Yeto Vellipoyindhi Manasu (2012). This success was followed by several unsuccessful films.
